Projects

Temporal and Spatial Domain Assistance Framework for Children with Autism

In this project, we (Satya K. Budumuru, Tarun Kota under guidance of Tapan K. Gandhi at IIT Delhi) created a framework through Magical World Hypothesis, which given a problem, develop a wikipedia for Children with Autism to help them learn faster.

To test the hypothesis and working of the framework, we developed two games, one in temporal domain and other in spatial domain and generated their wikipedia, which can understand (predict) player’s abilities and give levels based on his knowledge. Read more …

Currently, we are collecting data from Action for Autism and AIIMS.
